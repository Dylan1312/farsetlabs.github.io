'Event Round-up: Liberty IT Hackathon & Gathering of Sleighbells' tags: [] status: draft type: post published: false meta: _edit_last: '6' --- December got off to an energetic start with two concurrent events on the first weekend. It was all about project demos at the last Gathering of Lightning event of the year, followed by the Hack for Your Community hackathon sponsored by Liberty IT. We love hackathons. We love what they stand for and what they can achieve, and the fact that you have to be a little insane to undertake them. It's a big ask, to take a day or two out of your social life to enslave yourself to a high-speed, sleepless slog at a project that might not even work at the end of the session. It's been hard to get commitment from a good number of hackers for such events, as many other hackathon organisers have found. The turn-out this time was disappointing, especially against an encouraging number of registrations, so we're contemplating what improvements are needed. Any thoughts would be most welcome.
