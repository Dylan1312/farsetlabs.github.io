Hot-Desking


Farset Labs is a great place to get some work done in the city, or a place to meet up with colleagues and friends, and with on site tea/coffee facilities, 150Mbps fibre internet, and access to our prototyping workshops, Farset Labs is a great place to develop, create, and collaborate.

Please sign the guestbook at reception when you come in!

Benefits

We’re funded by the hackers, developers, creatives, inventors and entrepreneurs that use the space every day for their ideas, experiments, and projects.

  • Belonging to an accepting and creative community
  • Escaping to an inspiring space
  • 150Mbps FTTP Internet connection & WiFi provided by Tibus
  • Networking with the best and brightest innovators and creators in Belfast
  • Communal electronics equipment, tools, and components
  • 3D prototyping facilities
  • Kitted-out workshop with a growing selection of hand tools & machines

All Day Pass dues go towards the core operation of the space; events and projects are normally funded by additional donations / sponsorships
